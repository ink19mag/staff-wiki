This wiki is a collection of important information and procedures for the operation of _Ink 19_, an all-volunteer publication that has covered music, film, books, performance, and the culture around them since 1991. It is intended for use by staff members and contributors to the site; if you would like to join us, you can [apply here](https://ink19.com/staff/join-us).

Start with the [Staff Guide](editorial/staff-guide) for our mission and values, then move on to [Editorial Sections](editorial/editorial-sections) and [Post an Article](editorial/post-an-article).

If you can't find what you are looking for, please [contact us](mailto:editors@ink19.com?subject=Staff%20Wiki) and let us know.
