## Our Mission

_Ink 19_ has published original, honest, human writing about music, film, books, performance, and the culture around them since 1991. We're an all-volunteer publication powered by writers, artists, photographers, and other contributors covering the work that matters to them, without waiting for assignments or pitching for permission. We exist to give those voices a place to develop a craft, participate in an editorial framework, find an audience, and add to a creative community.


## What We Value

- **Honest, never cheap.** We tell the truth about the work, we don't take shots for sport.
- **Human voices.** Real people, real reactions, real reporting. AI is a tool, never a byline. (See our [AI + LLM Policy](../ai-policy).)
- **Original.** Plagiarism, fabricated quotes, and recycled work fail us and the artists we cover. Everything we publish is the contributor's own.
- **Writer-driven.** _Ink 19_ runs on the obsessions of its contributors. The [Opportunities](https://staff.ink19.com/opportunities/) list exists to serve that. It's deep enough that you can usually find what you want to cover, and if you can't, the editors will add it for you. You don't have to pitch for permission; you have to know what you care about.
- **Open by genre and by scale.** No editorial bias. Basement shows, major releases, the band your friend's cousin is in — what matters is that it's interesting and the writer cares.
- **Respectful.** Of artists, sources, readers, venues, publicists, and each other. Inclusive language, correct names, preferred pronouns.
- **Volunteer and proud.** We're not a profit-seeking organization and we're not paying anyone — and we don't accept payment from the artists and labels we cover. (See our [Compensation Policy](../compensation-policy).)


## Who We Are

Our Publisher / Owner, Ian Koss, focuses on tech and vision. Editor Rose Petralia manages day-to-day operations and staff onboarding. Frank Dreyer designs our looks. Beyond that, _Ink 19_ is the writers, photographers, designers, and creators who choose to work with us. Some of us have been here since 1991; others joined last week.

Get in touch [here](https://ink19.com/staff).


## How We Work

We coordinate with publicists and artists to bring new music, films, books, shows, and other media to our contributors, and then we publish their corresponding work at ink19.com. Advances are typically streaming or downloaded, strictly for the reviewer's use only and not to be shared. Digital and physical review materials are yours to keep.

Over time, our writers become recognized as Trusted Voices — that's exactly what we want. We reserve the right to reject any work that does not meet editorial standards, and may return stories for corrections or tightening up. We're mindful that our staff is volunteer; we're equally committed to publishing work all of us can take pride in.


## About Your Privacy

Requests for your information are forwarded to you. Your name, city-state-country, and byline information may be seen by visitors to the site. Your personal information is only shared with known publicists for the coordination of physical materials.
